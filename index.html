FAQs

Who can participate ?
#code is open for all engineering students, professionals and also anyone with minimum programming experience.

Is it a team or individual event ?
You could sign up as an individual or a team. We are allowing team sizes to be a maximum of 3 members per team.

Will the judging criteria be the same for both ?
The judging criteria is highly centred around your employability. The more your solutions impress, the higher score you get.

Can I leave early?
You are allowed to leave after you submit. Results will be announced according to schedule. Although we insist you to stay for all the mini events that will take place throughout the 24 hours.

What's the format for #code ?
#Code 2015 is an open platform hackathon. Hacks will be judged based on creativity, technicality, finish and usefulness.

What should I bring ?
All participants are required to get their valid identities for admission (name must match registrations ). You will need to get your own laptops, chargers and any kind of harware that you will be using. Oh yeah don't forget to get your toothbrush.

What about food and drinks?
Worry not, food and drinks will be provided to make sure you get through the event with minimum attrition.

Will there be internet connection ?
Yes. Internet connection will be provided for every team.

What is the Registration fee?
150 INR per team.

Who owns the intellectual property on the work that was built at the event ?
The developer owns all intellectual property. As open source enthusiats ourselves, we do suggest open sourcing it.
